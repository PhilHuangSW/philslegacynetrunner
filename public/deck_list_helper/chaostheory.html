Intro:  Chaos Theory is a clever girl, a wunderkind. She is the classic big Shaper build; powerful icebreakers along with great ways to generate credits. She utilizes MU as a weapon rather than just space for programs.

Strengths:  Huge breaker suite and a literal printer of credits

Weaknesses:  Slow to setup, everything is expensive

Playstyle:  To make use of her passive, we've incorporated all the Icebreakers that do well with large amounts of MU. Savant and Adept along with the incredible Overmind will allow you to break pretty much any ICE you encounter. Hosting all your programs onto Dhegdheers and Dinosaurus will ensure you have the maximum strength/counters for your Icebreakers.
The downfall to this deck is definitely in the setup. Everything is quite costly to build and the economy package, while endless, can take a little while to get going. If the corp catches on and can out-tempo you by advancing agendas before you can get set up, it can sometimes be difficult to mount a comeback.
Take your first couple of turns setting up your economy. Once the corp threatens to advance out an agenda, all you need is a Self-modifying Code/Overmind to easily break through and steal agendas. Allowing the corp to score a couple of agendas is okay, just be on the lookout for a timely Deep Data Mining run to put a lock on R&D.

Mulligan:  Try to look for Overmind or Self-modifying Code with some economy such as Lucky Find or Data Foldings.

Tips:  Ideal rig would consist of Magnum Opus and Adept/Savant onto Dhegdheers with the other on Dinosaurus. Don't be afraid to install programs/icebreakers for fear of using your MU if it means pressuring the corp. Just Scavenge it later onto a Dhegdheer to free up that space!
