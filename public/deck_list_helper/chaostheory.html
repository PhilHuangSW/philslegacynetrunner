Intro:  Chaos Theory was one of my favorite runners when she was first introduced. It was rare to find an ID with a minimum decklist of 40 cards at that time. Her passive ability to have an extra MU will be used very well with her rig!

Strengths:  Huge breaker suite and near endless economy

Weaknesses:  Slow to setup, everything is expensive

Playstyle:  To make use of her extra MU, we've brought in the best Icebreakers that utilize extra Memory. Savant and Adept along with the incredible Overmind will allow you to break pretty much any ICE you encounter. Hosting all your programs onto Dhegdheers and Dinosaurus will ensure you have the maximum strength/counters for your Icebreakers.
The downfall to this deck is definitely in the setup. Everything is quite costly to build and the economy package, while good, can take a little while to get going. If the corp catches on and can out-tempo you by advancing agendas before you can get set up, it can sometimes be difficult to mount a comeback.
Take your first couple of turns setting up your economy. Appear weak and have the corp threaten to advance out an agenda. All you need is a Self-modifying Code/Overmind to easily break through and steal agendas. Once you have tempo back on your side, continue to economy up.

Mulligan:  Try to look for Overmind, Magnum Opus, or Self-modifying Code with some economy as a starting hand. Don't be afraid of using all your cards if you have a Game Day in your hand, easy refuel and helps with Power Naps!

Tips:  Ideal rig would consist of Magnum Opus and Adept or Savant onto Dhegdheers with the other on Dinosaurus, 3x LLDS Memory Diamond and 3x Data Folding for a total of 9-12 strength breakers with 3 credits at start of turn, and endless econ using Magnum Opus.
