Intro:  Khan is a straight shooter. Her objectives are clear: make lots of credits and bust through the corp's defenses.

Strengths:  Incredible credits package, strong HQ pressure

Weaknesses:  Icebreakers are not efficient, can burn through a large credit pool very quickly

Playstyle:  Khan is very straight forward in her approach. High-Stakes Job, Temujin Contract, and Bank Job all generate you a boat load of credits for you to break through to HQ using your birds (Golden - Peregrine - Saker).
There is a circular gameplan you can utilize with Khan's ability, Autoscripters, and the birds. When appropriate, break all the subroutines of an ICE with a bird, use the last paid ability of the birds to derez the ICE, use Khan's ability to bring back the bird at a 1-credit discount, then receive your click from Autoscripter. It is extremely expensive to do, but when used on a high-rez ICE, it can save you a lot of credits in the long run.
The most difficult part of playing the deck will be to find when to execute a SYN Attack to maximize scoring potential. The *usual* best time to play SYN Attack will be when the corp has 2-4 cards. This usually puts the corp in the most difficult positions on whether to draw or discard.

Mulligan:  Bank Jobs, Temujin Contracts, and Special Orders are all good cards to keep.

Tips:  As a general rule of thumb, using any of the birds' (Golden - Peregrine - Saker) last paid ability on a high rez cost ICE will be a good play. Using them when the corp is low on credits can also net you a couple of free accesses on HQ or R&D.
