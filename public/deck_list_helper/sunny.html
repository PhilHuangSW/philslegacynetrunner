Intro:  Sunny works for the best security company in the world: Globalsec. With the help of their unlimited resources, Sunny gets access to all the biggest and baddest tech currently in the market to run where she pleases. She, of course, gets compensated generously for her expertise in the field.

Strengths:  Insane link potential, corps will never be able to trace her. Has the potential to print boat loads of credits (18 credits in one turn!)

Weaknesses:  Huge deck size, slow to setup, no special ID ability

Playstyle:  Sunny doesn't really do anything fancy against the corp, she simply brute forces her way through with the insane amount of resources she has access to. Underworld Contacts for drip economy, Rachel Beckman gaining her an extra click, and her suite of the best programs make Sunny an absolute force to be reckoned with.
Setup your rig as quickly as possible using Earthrise Hotel and Symmetrical Visage to cycle through your big deck, then take control of the late game by breaking through any server. Another Day, Another Paycheck will help ensure you stay healthy in credits when you're catching up in points from the slow start.

Mulligan:  Underworld Contacts, Security Nexus, and Symmetrical Visage are all great cards to keep. Rabbit Hole will help filter out your deck and help with making Security Nexus essentially a free bypass ICE every turn.

Tips:  Snitch and Au Revoir are insane together. If you're lucky enough to get all three, you can run any empty server or unrezzed ICE and then jack out to gain 3 credits. Doing that for all 4 clicks (5 with Rachel), nets you 12 credits!
