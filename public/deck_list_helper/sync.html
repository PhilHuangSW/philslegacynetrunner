Intro:  SYNC is the reason NBN is part of the big four mega corporations. This web of interconnectivity has made everything viewable from your very own PAD, including all the sneaking around the runner does. It is quite hard to make friends when there's a camera pointed at you in every. single. direction.

Strengths:  Lots of tagging capabilities, tag punishment is plentiful

Weaknesses:  Weak if the runner can continually shake tags

Playstyle:  SYNC really wants to land and keep tags on the runner. A lot of different things activate when the runner is tagged such as Closed Accounts, Psychographics, Market Research, and Quantum Predictive Model.
Timing when to fire a Hard-Hitting News can really make the runner unable to shake all the tags. With SYNC, it'll cost the runner 12 credits to get rid of 4 tags, which will almost guarantee an open scoring window for you to capitalize on.
Laying out an Explode-a-palooza just for the runner to score it can make for an absolutely brutal Midseason Replacements tag storm. Keep the runner tagged all the time and you should have no problems closing out the game.

Mulligan:  Finding Data Ravens in your opening hand can absolutely stop further access to your central servers since the runner will be scared of the upcoming tag punishment.

Tips:  If the runner decides to float tags, take advantage of Psychographics and Project Beale and score as many points as you can afford. Sometimes it'll just straight win you the game if you're 3-4 points away by placing 5 or 7 advancements respectively.
Allowing the runner to score 15 Minutes or Explode-a-palooza to later hit them with a Midseason Replacements is a great way to take over the game.
