Intro:  Nasir is a pretty weird runner. He has a lot of tools at his disposal, but finding the right one for the job can sometimes take a little while. Time is his best friend though, at least when it comes to working in his Personal Workshop.

Strengths:  Free economy with his ID, has a tool for almost every situation

Weaknesses:  Econ is extremely swingy and unpredictable, smart ICE placement can completely lock you out of a server

Playstyle:  The most important card in this deck is the Personal Workshop. Once you have it online (or multiple online), just chuck all your hardware and programs onto it. Then all you need to do is run headstrong into corp ICE to trigger your ability for free money!
The way it works is before your ability kicks in, you have a timing window to spend all your credits into Personal Workshop supplies, then once you're done spending as much as you can, trigger your ability for easy money.
Chameleon will be your first icebreaker to look for to place onto the workshop. It is flexible for you in breaking whatever you encounter. Pair that with 3x LLDS Processors and that's a 6 strength icebreaker of your choice! With the help of Replicator, you should have no problem installing all your processors when needed the most.
Kati Jones will be another key piece for your economy. Getting free credits from the workshop and your ID, while nice, is sometimes unpredictable. Kati will help keep you healthy in credits when the corp has rezzed a low cost ICE that empties your credit pool.

Mulligan:  Hard mull for Personal Workshop and Kati Jones. They are the 2 most important pieces in the deck and having them are vital to Nasir's success

Tips:  Crescentus and Emergency Shutdown are incredible cards to help you take more advantage of Nasir's ability.
Using London Library on Chameleons are a great way to save on 2 credits when money is tight. Due to the timing structure, you'll be able to pull Chameleons back into your hand before having to trash it from London Library's effect.
