Intro:  Reina is a Grandmaster when it comes to running. With her trusty chess pieces, she infiltrates the corp through slow, methodical movement.

Strengths:  Strong economy engine, strong endgame pressure and presence

Weaknesses:  Lots of moving pieces that might rely on other pieces to work, can be a little overwhelming on what to do next

Playstyle:  Reina is a sight to behold when she's firing from all cylinders. Her basic economy engine relies on Professional Contacts with the help of a couple Liberated Accounts. Setting up Scheherazade and Deep Red alongside her trusty chess pieces (Pawn - Rook - Bishop) is another wonderful source of economy while providing ICE debilitating utility for your icebreakers.
Gameplay will involve clicking Professional Contacts a couple of times then installing your pieces. Being able to install chess pieces onto Scheherazade then immediately moving them onto ICE via Deep Red is incredibly satisfying when building up your rig.

Mulligan:  Look for Professional Contacts and Xanadu in your opening hand.

Tips:  Overdrawing with Professional Contacts and discarding Morning Star only to then do a Retrieval Run for it is a very satisfying play.
