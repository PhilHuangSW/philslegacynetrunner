Intro:  Weyland Consortium is one of the most openly brutal mega corporations in all of netrunner. They build giant constellations of ICE to ensure runners can't touch their secrets, but in the cases where the runner gets lucky, there are always countermeasures to ensure none of it gets to the public.

Strengths:  Easy chip damage, strong advanceable ICE

Weaknesses:  Slow gameplay, spending credits and clicks to advance ICE means not advancing agendas

Playstyle:  Weyland definitely prefers a slower and longer playstyle. The deck only contains 8 agendas, so a lot of your turns will just be to simply build up your advanceable ICE until the runner cannot penetrate your defenses.
Punitive Counterstrike can sometimes win you the game if the runner isn't careful enough. By taking damage from your ID, if they accidentally steal one too many agendas, it might lead to a flatlined runner if you have counterstrike in hand.
Even after advancements have been placed, you can still use Trick of Light and Commercialization to benefit from the lost clicks and credits sunk into the ICE investment.

Mulligan:  Try to look for an Ice Wall to hide agendas behind. Being able to score a Cleaners turn 3-4 can severely hinder the runners progress and gameplan.

Tips:  Use Anson Rose on the likes of Tyrant and Woodcutter to immediately make use of their subroutines once rezzed.
Don't be afraid of using Punitive Counterstrike even if it doesn't flatline the runner. Stopping their rig building process can allow you to score behind your beefy ICE.
Space Camp triggers from anywhere (even Archives!), so make sure to always trigger it when it has been accessed.
Tyrant and Woodcutter are the only ICE that cannot be advanced when unrezzed, every other piece of ICE is advanceable. Make use of this to trigger your ID as much as possible!
