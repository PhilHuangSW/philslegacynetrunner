Intro:  Smoke is my absolute favorite runner. She's flashy, she's cocky, she's efficient. Her stealth breaker suite makes running a 4-ICE deep server seem like a walk in the park.

Strengths:  Runs are cheap, can do crazy/flashy things like scoring Notoriety and taking an extra turn by having an Encore

Weaknesses:  Can be locked out of servers if stealth credits are spent unwisely, highly dependent on stealth credits

Playstyle:  Smoke takes a little bit of finesse to play as it's not really her game to gain a whole lot of credits and then run everywhere. Make use of her recurring stealth credits by making cheap runs every turn on a lightly defended HQ or R&D.
Using your Patrons on naked servers is a great way to quickly build up your rig and using Out of the Ashes when you can afford to will be great for future turns where you are seeking to score Notoriety and/or to have an Encore.
Algo Trading might seem slow (and it is), but with your recurring credits allowing you to continue running while investing your actual credits will help in your future turns.

Mulligan:  Try to look for Patron and some Daily Casts for your draw engine and drip economy respectively. Having Beth Kilrain-Chang in your opening hand never hurts as well.

Tips:  Using Cold Read on naked Assets with high trash costs is a great use of a card when you're low on credits, and since you didn't have to use any Icebreakers, nothing will get trashed.
Using Out of the Ashes on non-pressure situations can really help future you when you really need that extra run on a turn to secure the victory.
