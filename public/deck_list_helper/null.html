Intro:  Null is jaded. He worked for corporations and saw firsthand all the bs they got away with. He has now vowed to try and burn everything the corp has built.

Strengths:  Ways to trash anything and everything the corp can build

Weaknesses:  No reliable way to pump up icebreakers, no good credit engine

Playstyle:  Null's goal is to destroy everything the corp has ever built. With the help of Parasite, Forked, Knifed, and Spooned, ICE will just melt away forcing the corp to spend and install more ICE and not being able to realize their investment.
Aside from ICE destruction, Wanton Destruction, Gravedigger, and Imp all give you a way of dealing with cards that aren't normally able to be dealt with (such as operations) in a fairly cheap manner.
Break down the corp one card at a time and secure the victory once they have absolutely nothing left.

Mulligan:  Datasuckers are an important piece to shore up some of Null's weaknesses. Sifr and the slow-growing icebreakers (Nfr - Sunya) are also great keeps as they take a little while to get going.

Tips:  Imp-ing an Agenda can be a strong play sometimes when an effect triggering off scoring would be deadly.
Don't be afraid to trash your Black Orchestra as its ability can still be used from the heap to bring it back into play.
Finding a time to do an Amped Up Wanton Destruction run is very satisfying (and sometimes game-winning) to do.
