Intro:  No one is quite sure where Apex came from, all we do know is that it is hungry, and always on the hunt for its next prey.

Strengths:  Everything and anything can be used as fuel

Weaknesses:  Can run out of answers if the wrong cards are installed facedown

Playstyle:  Knowing what resources to feed Endless Hunger will determine your success. Figuring out which cards to install facedown and which to use is an important skill to learn when playing Apex. The Three Headed Cerberus will help you deal with ICE that can't be handled with Endless Hunger. When you've spent all the counters on the dogs, you can either use them as food or to Uninstall them and install them again for fresh counters.
Trope and Deja Vu will be your only means of recurring your used cards, use them wisely!
There's no multi-access unfortunately, but we do have Apocalypse. Obliterate everything they own, then run amok on all servers while the corp scrambles to try and rebuild itself.

Mulligan:  There is no must-have card in your opening hand. Process Automation will keep you healthy in credits and cards, while Endless Hunger will break almost anything, so those could be good keeps.

Tips:  Don't wait too long to use Apocalypse. Using one when the corp is just starting to get into groove can severely hinder their agenda. Playing Apex is all about tempo; never let the corp get an upper hand, always keep the pressure.
